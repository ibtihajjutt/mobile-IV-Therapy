Brand Positioning
"Medical-Grade Wellness, Delivered to Your Door"
The brand sits at the intersection of healthcare credibility and luxury lifestyle service. It's not a hospital—it's a spa experience with clinical precision.
Brand Personality

Trustworthy: Medical professionals, certifications, transparent ingredients
Aspirational: High-end imagery, premium pricing, celebrity clientele
Convenient: On-demand service, seamless booking, minimal friction
Empowering: Take control of your health proactively, not reactively

Voice & Tone
Do's

Use second person ("You deserve to feel your best")
Be confident but not pushy ("Trusted by 150,000+ clients")
Educate without overwhelming ("Here's exactly what's in our Immunity IV")
Emphasize results ("Feel better in 30-60 minutes")

Don'ts

Avoid medical jargon without explanation
Don't use fear-based marketing ("Don't let dehydration ruin your day" ❌)
Never make unverified health claims
Avoid overly clinical or sterile language

Example Copy
Good: "Rehydrate, recover, and feel like yourself again—all from the comfort of home."
Bad: "Our isotonic solution provides rapid cellular rehydration."

Imagery Guidelines
Photo Style

Natural lighting, never harsh clinical lighting
Settings: Modern homes, upscale hotels, clean offices
Models: Diverse ages/ethnicities, wearing comfortable casual wear
Nurses: Professional scrubs (not hospital scrubs), clean modern aesthetic
IV Bags: Artfully photographed, never clinical or scary

What to Avoid

Hospital beds, medical equipment clutter
Needles in arms (show the bag, not the insertion)
Overly edited/filtered photos
Stock photos that feel generic
